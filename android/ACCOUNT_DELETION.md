# Account Deletion Request - Home Guide

To comply with Google Play policies, Home Guide provides a clear way for users to request the deletion of their account and all associated data.

## How to request account deletion
If you wish to delete your account and remove your personal information from our system, please follow these steps:

1.  **In-App Option:** Go to your Profile/Settings section and select "Delete Account".
2.  **Email Request:** Send an email to **[TU_EMAIL_AQUI]** with the subject "Account Deletion Request".
3.  **Verification:** For security reasons, we will send a confirmation code to your registered email address to verify your identity.

## Data that will be deleted
Upon successful verification, the following data will be permanently removed from our active servers within 30 days:
*   User profile information (name, email, organization).
*   Your personal task history and uploaded photos.
*   Device identifiers associated with your account.

## Data Retention
Some data may be retained for a limited period if required by law or for legitimate business purposes (e.g., financial records if applicable), as detailed in our full Privacy Policy.
