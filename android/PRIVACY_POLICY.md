# Privacy Policy for Home Guide

**Last updated: March 19, 2024**

Home Guide ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is collected, used, and disclosed by Home Guide.

## 1. Information We Collect
*   **Location Data:** We collect precise and approximate location data to show you landscape management tasks near your current position. This is only used while the app is in use.
*   **Images:** If you upload photos of property maintenance, these are stored on our servers to document work progress.
*   **Device Identifiers:** We may collect basic device info for app functionality and notifications.

## 2. How We Use Information
We use the information to:
*   Provide property management services.
*   Document task completion.
*   Improve app performance.

## 3. Data Sharing
We do NOT sell your data. Data is only shared with your property management organization.

## 4. User Rights
You can request the deletion of your account and data at any time by contacting our support email.
